Dosa Recipe

Description:

Dosa is a traditional South Indian dish made from a fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter, spread thin and crisp on a hot griddle, creating a golden, savory crepe typically served with coconut chutney and sambar for a flavorful and wholesome meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups uncooked long-grain white or basmati rice
  • Filtered water, for soaking
  • 1/2 cup skinned whole urad gota (dried whole matpe beans), or skinned urad dal (dried split matpe beans)
  • 2 tablespoons chana dal (dried split chickpeas)
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 cup cooked basmati rice (optional)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • Ghee or canola oil
  • 1 recipe Potato Palya, for filling (optional)
  • For serving: chutney and sambar, or chutney pudi and plain yogurt.

Steps Of Making:

  1. Soak the rice.
  2. Soak the urad gota, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds.
  3. Drain the urad gota, chana dal, and fenugreek seeds.
  4. Blend the urad mixture.
  5. Drain the rice. .
  6. Blend the rice.
  7. Mix the blended rice and dal.
  8. Ferment the batter.
  9. Check that the batter has fermented.

COOKING THE DOSA:

  1. Stir the batter.
  2. Prepare for cooking.
  3. Heat a skillet with ghee.
  4. Pour in batter.
  5. Spread the batter.
  6. Add ghee to the edges and top of dosa.
  7. Cook the dosa.
  8. Flip the dosa.
  9. Fold the dosa.
  10. Or fill and fold the dosa.
  11. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  12. Serve the dosas.
