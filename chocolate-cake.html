Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description:

A chocolate cake is a rich and moist dessert that is popular for its deep chocolate flavor and versatility. It can be made in various forms, including layer cakes, sheet cakes, or cupcakes. Below is a detailed recipe for a classic chocolate cake.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups (240g)
  • Granulated sugar: 2 cups (400g)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder: ½ cup (50g)
  • Baking powder: 1½ teaspoons
  • Baking soda: 1½ teaspoons
  • Milk: 1 cup (240mL)
  • Vanilla extract: 1 tablespoon
  • Boiling water: 1 cup (240ml)
  • Additional unsweetened cocoa powder: ¼ cup (25g)

steps of making:

  1. Prep. Preheat the oven to 350º F.
  2. Whisk dry ingredients.
  3. Mix in wet ingredients
  4. Bake.
  5. Frost
