Samosa Recipe

A samosa is a fried South Asian and West Asian snack. It is a pastry with a savory filling that mostly consists of vegetables like spiced potatoes, onions, and peas, but can also include meat or fish, or even cheese.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour: 2 cups (250 grams)
  • Carom seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Ghee: 6 tablespoons (50 grams)
  • Water: 7 to 8 tablespoons (or as required)
  • Potatoes: 3 medium (300 to 350 grams)
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon (I used mustard oil)
  • Cumin seeds: ½ teaspoon
  • Ginger: 1 teaspoon (finely chopped)
  • Green chillies: 2 teaspoons (finely chopped)
  • Red Chili Powder: ½ teaspoon

Steps OF Making:

  1. The spiced potato filling;
  2. The Samosa dough;
  3. Making the Samosa parcels; and
  4. Frying – Sorry, there is no alternative! Do not try to bake them, you will be sorely disappointed!
